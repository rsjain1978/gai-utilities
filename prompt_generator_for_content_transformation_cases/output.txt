Fidelity calculates fees based on your total investment value, offering transparency and flexible options. **Service fees** include account administration and investment tools, starting at **0.35% annually** for under £250,000, reducing to **0.20%** for larger investments. Fees are capped for shares at **£90 per year**. **Dealing fees** apply only to shares, such as **£7.50 online** or **£1.50 for regular plans**. There are no charges for junior accounts, cash holdings, or fund transactions. Investment charges, set by fund managers, start as low as **0.05%**. Fees are collected via a Cash Management Account. Fidelity provides clear, detailed breakdowns for full transparency.
